🗓️ 90-Day Plan
Day 1-14
Set up sending domains, warm up inboxes. Add 40 prospects. Send first 20 cold emails.
Day 15-30
Ramp to 10 emails/day. First replies. Book 2–3 calls. Close first client ($2K–3K/mo).
Day 31-60
Scale to 20 emails/day. 2–3 more clients. $5K–8K MRR. Build campaigns for each.
Day 61-90
4–5 clients. $10K+ MRR. Systemize. Ask every client for a referral.
