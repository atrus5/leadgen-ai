Stage 06 · Niche-tuned outreach

Every niche has its own opener hooks, vocabulary, and followup cadence.

Two-step composition

  1. Template (always runs): the niche DNA in config/niches.json provides the opener, followup pain, taboo-word list, and tone.
  2. Llama 3 refinement (only if Ollama is live): the local LLM re-phrases the opener to read like a real person wrote it.

If Ollama isn't running, the templated body is the final body. That's intentional — it keeps the system useful when your VPS is rebuilding the model.

The 24 niches we ship with

Roofing, Real Estate, Dental, HVAC, Legal, Mortgage, Insurance, Fitness, Plumbing, Duct Cleaning, Gutters, Snow Removal, Grass & Lawn, Tree Service, Care Aid, Mechanic, Cleaning Service, Pest Control, Pool Service, Painting, Pressure Washing, Electrician, Chiropractic, Retail.

What's in the niche DNA record

{
  "key": "Plumbing",
  "tone": "neighborly, plain-spoken, trustworthy",
  "vocabulary": ["water heater","tankless","drain","leak","burst","sewer line"],
  "taboo_words": ["synergy","transformative","paradigm"],
  "opener_hooks": [
    "{firstName}, half the calls {businessName} is missing in {city} are on Sunday at 8am...",
    "If {businessName} doesn't have an after-hours intake flow, ~60% of those calls are going to a competitor every weekend..."
  ],
  "followup_pain": "truck-roll economics — every missed dispatch is lost margin",
  "followup_hook": "Water heater replacements this fall — what's your top-of-mind bottleneck now?"
}

Why use taboos

Cold emails full of "synergize", "transformative", "best-in-class" go straight to spam filters and out-of-brain filters. Each niche defines its own taboo list so the renderer scrubs them automatically.

Adding a new niche

  1. Open config/niches.json.
  2. Append a new entry under "niches": [].
  3. Reload the service (Flask reads the file at startup; for hot-reload, restart the process).

How the LLM prompt is shaped

system: You are a cold-email copywriter for local-service businesses.
        You produce 4-6 sentence cold emails. Tone: plain-spoken,
        never salesy, never pressuring. Hard rule: do not invent facts
        about the recipient; only rephrase what you were given.
        Hard rule: under 90 words total.

user:   Niche: Plumbing
        Recipient business: Smith Plumbing
        Recipient contact (may be empty): Mike
        City: Austin
        Customer-lifetime-value estimate: $2K–8K per job

        Rewrite the email body to read like it was written by a real person
        for a real local owner-operator. Keep the core hook. Output ONLY the body.

Followups and breakups

Two followups ship by default: day 3 and day 7. On top of the niche opener, they re-use followup_pain and followup_hook from the DNA record. To add a 4-touch sequence, edit outreach/generator.py and add new step handling.
