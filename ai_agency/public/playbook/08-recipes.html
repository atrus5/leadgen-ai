How to override a recipe

Two paths:

  1. Edit config/niches.json directly. Restart the Flask process when you want the new copy loaded.
  2. Programmatic: api/api/settings never overrides niches — they're code-as-content. Use a small script to rebuild the file from an Airtable / Google Sheet export.
