Stage 05 · Morning brief

A single email arrives at your configured time with the new prospects you have to act on today.

What's in the email

  • Total new prospects inserted since 00:00, grouped by client.
  • Source attribution (Apollo vs Places) — useful for deciding where to invest.
  • A one-line "Open LeadGen AI" CTA.

What you actually do

  1. Open the wizard UI in a browser.
  2. Click Prospects. Filter by status = new.
  3. For each prospect with a contact email, click "Generate cold email" → review the niche-tuned opener → click "Send".
  4. For prospects without a contact email (often from Places), open their website and either:
    • Copy the contact form URL into the prospect's notes field, or
    • Mark them no_email on the database so they're not re-targetted tomorrow.
  5. For prospects with poor score (<4), keep them in the list — they'll become the slow-pipeline batch.

Daily checklist (rough time per)

  • Review new prospects (15 min)
  • Send 8–12 cold emails (25 min)
  • Reply to anyone whose reply came in overnight (15 min)
  • Update stale prospects (5 min)

Send your first manual email

curl -X POST http://your-host/api/email/send \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "prospect_id": "abc123...",
    "step": "cold"
  }'

The system will look up the prospect, generate a niche-tuned cold email via the templates + Llama 3 (if running), and send it via SMTP — respecting the daily quota.

Sending checks

  • Quota exceeded? Wait until tomorrow, or move the message to the slow queue.
  • Send returns duplicate_step? You already sent this step to this prospect in the last 7 days. Pick a different prospect.
  • Send errors with smtp_auth? Test SMTP from the wizard — credentials probably rotated.
