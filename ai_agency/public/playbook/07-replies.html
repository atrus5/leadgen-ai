Stage 07 · Reply handling

Inbound replies are auto-classified and HOT ones get forwarded to the paying client.

The reply poller

Every 15 minutes the scheduler runs outreach/reply_parser.py:

  1. Open an IMAP_SSL connection to your mailbox.
  2. Fetch up to 25 UNSEEN messages.
  3. Decode headers + bodies (text/plain preferred, falls back to text/html stripped of tags).
  4. Match each sender's email to a prospect in our prospects table.
  5. Classify intent via Llama 3 if running, else deterministic keyword fallback.
  6. Persist to replies table.

The seven labels

  • HOT — clear interest, wants a call/quote/meeting. Auto-forwarded.
  • MORE_INFO — wants details but not yet ready to buy. Auto-forwarded.
  • NOT_NOW — polite deferral. Stays in the queue.
  • OUT_OF_OFFICE — auto reply. Ignored.
  • UNSUBSCRIBE — angry / stop. Add to blacklist, never email again.
  • NOISE — unrelated. Stays in the queue.

The forwarder

When the label is HOT or MORE_INFO, outreach/forwarder.py:

  1. Looks up the prospect + paying client from the database.
  2. Composes a brief: business name, contact, email, phone, website, niche, original reply.
  3. Sends via SMTP using the same credentials as outbound.
  4. Marks replies.forwarded = 1 so it never re-sends.

The forward body

Hi Mike,

A prospect of yours (Sunrise Plumbing, Austin) just raised their hand
and asked for more information. The full transcript is below.

— LEAD BRIEF —
Business:  Sunrise Plumbing
Contact:   John Davis  (Owner)
Email:     john@sunriseplumbing.com
Phone:     (555) 123-4567
Website:   sunriseplumbing.com
Niche:     Plumbing

— ORIGINAL REPLY —
Subject: Re: 5-min read?
Received: 2025-01-23T15:22:10+00:00

Yes — please send more info. We're losing 30% of after-hours calls
and would love to see your intake flow.

— LeadGen AI

Caution: don't leak your list

The forward message only contains the one prospect whose reply was HOT. The rest of the campaign and the other prospects stay out of the forwarded email. The agency operator should never be in a position to receive more than what comes from a single reply.

Manual reply check

curl -X POST http://your-host/api/jobs/run \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"name":"reply_poller"}'
